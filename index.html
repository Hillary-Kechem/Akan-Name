Akan Names

In Ghanian culture, many factors are at work in the naming process and a seemingly simple name can hold someone’s entire biography. A Ghanian name is much more than a simple, functional tag to identify someone, it is a symbol, an emblem. A name can shape a person’s character, mold their social identity, and even influence their destiny. The meaning attached to a name will determine much about the present and the future of a child.
